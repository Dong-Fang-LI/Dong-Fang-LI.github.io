Automatic Cell Manipulation and Patterning System by using Dielectrophoresis:

My research in Ph.D. is on cell manipulation and patterning with a robotic-aided automatic system. Equipped with a microchip that can generate electric fields, the system enables biological cell translation and orientation via the DEP force.
