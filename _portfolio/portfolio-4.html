Equipped with a large-scale quadrupole microchip, the system enables mouse embryo alignment and rotation for laser drilling.

collection: portfolio ---

Equipped with a large-scale quadrupole microchip, the system enables mouse embryo translation and rotation. Images are extracted to obtain the position and orientation information for the embryo. With this information, the PID controller is applied to the system for precise embryo manipulation.
